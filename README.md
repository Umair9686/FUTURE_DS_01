📊 FUTURE_DS_01 – Superstore Sales Performance Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

FUTURE_DS_01 is the first task completed as part of the Data Science & Analytics Internship Program.

The objective of this project is to analyze Superstore sales data and transform raw business data into meaningful insights using Power BI.

This dashboard helps stakeholders understand sales performance, product trends, regional performance, and profitability drivers to support better business decisions.

🎯 Business Questions Addressed

This analysis focuses on answering key business questions:

Which products generate the highest revenue?

How do sales and profit change over time?

Which categories and regions are most profitable?

How do discounts impact overall profitability?

Where should the business focus to improve performance and growth?

🧹 Data Preparation

Before building the dashboard, the dataset was prepared by:

Cleaning and structuring raw sales data

Removing inconsistencies and formatting issues

Creating calculated measures using DAX

Building KPI metrics for business performance analysis

📊 Dashboard Features

The interactive Power BI dashboard includes:

Executive KPI Overview

Total Sales

Total Profit

Total Orders

Total Quantity

Monthly Sales Trend Analysis

Top 5 Products by Sales

Sales and Profit Analysis by Sub-Category

Regional Sales Performance

Interactive Filters

Category

Region

Order Date

Business Insights Section

🔍 Key Insights

Key findings from the dashboard include:

The West region generates the highest sales revenue.

Certain top-performing products contribute significantly to total sales.

Technology category shows strong profit performance.

Sales show noticeable growth during later months of the year.

Analyzing top-performing products can help businesses focus on profitable segments.

DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Calculated measures and KPIs

Microsoft Excel – Data preparation and cleaning
